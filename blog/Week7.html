What are three ideas you might want to do for your final project?

I don't have a clear idea of what my final project should be at this point, but I think I'd like it to involve cooking/recipes, something my husband could use to make his online music theory classes easier to teach (now that we're under quarantine, but in the future as well), or something related to ballet. This may not be possible, but I personally would love to have (and therefore make) an app that allows the user to input specific ingredients and keywords (i.e. dietary restrictions) and have recipe options that don't require searching through lengthy blogs for the exact location of the recipe, only to find out that it doesn't meet all of my criteria in the first place.

Why do you see JavaScript useful or hard to use?

JavaScript is useful because it can be used for dynamic web pages and apps. It is hard to use because it involves a thorough knowledge of its tools as well as the logic of how to use them.

What aspects about JavaScript are you interested in learning more about?

I want to become more familiar with object oriented JS and various methods of dynamic rendering.
